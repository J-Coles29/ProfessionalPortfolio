MY JOURNEY

Hello and Welcome! I'm Jordan, a Leicstershire based web/software developer and Web/UI|UX designer. Feel free to read about my journey so far.

After multiple years of retail, I decided to set my sights on bigger things for myself and my daughter. I went to university to study Computer Science which included many programming languages from Haskell to C++ and began my love for functional-based programming; This also resparked my creativity as I realised that I can implement that into programming and website design in my own way. I decided to share my knowledge and skills as much as possible by working towards my teaching degree in Computer Science. This included teaching students from year 7 up to A-Level students;l My university lecturers were a big inspiration for this, the values and passsion of technology and software is so vast and everyone deserves a chance to delve into it, if they so please.

An Odessey is often used to describe a long, arduous journey; Unfortunatly that is what many of us go through in life but there is never any shame in it. After settling into my teaching position, a few years later I found myself at a serious uphill chapter, however, with it came a lesson; "Hope is not a sickness, It is the cure" - Shelby Mahurin. The Pandemic had hit, schools had closed, humanity sort of stood still. A series of very unfortunate events unfolded; A teacher no more and crawling out of homelessness, my best foot was put forward and the strength to get up, carry on and strive for more had returned. Don't give up, you matter more than you think ♥ Since this part of my journey, I am a very big advocator for mental health, mental illness and non-discrimination of both.
